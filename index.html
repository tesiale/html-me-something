Mountainside Adventure

Ha Ling Peak | Alberta, Canada | 2016

Starting Point...

Here we are, about to start our hike up Ha Ling Peak! A 4hr~ 5.6km trail in Canmore, Alberta, Canada.

Watch out for bears...

We were told this area does have the occasional bears roaming around, how scary!

Good thing we brought bear spray just in case..! Fortunately, we never had to use it.

Climbing up the trail...

Following the trailheads, we zigged and zagged around the forest... slowing making our ascent.

Halfway there...?

The trail eventually lead to a break from the forest trees.

This halfway point was where we decided to stop for a rest and just enjoy the view!


Out of the forest...

Forest eventually gave way to rocky gravelly terrain as we continued our trek above.

Almost there...!

A sigh of relief as we finally spot the summit of the trails.

It's been 3-hours and everyone's pushing their legs to keep going!

The Finishing Prize...!

View was worth the hike! Beautiful weather, beautiful town, beautiful mountains!



Now to make our descent...
